Future Plans

College

I hope to take my major in graphic design to new heights by networking and building a resume. After college I want to start with a company to continue the building of my resume to the point where I can finally feel good about freelancing. Freelancing will be my second step where I can continue to learn and grow in my field. Depending on how both of the first steps go I may jump back into one of them, but then I want to start doing photography. Overall there are a couple things that I want to try and get good at, then move on to the next thing and refine my skills more, just in a little different way.

Company

I want to start with joining a business for a couple of years in order to grow my skills and credentials as a graphic designer. This will also allow me the opportunity to see if I like working with a specific company. The plan is to do this for at least 5 years if not more.

Freelancing

The next step in my journey is to learn all I can while freelancing. I feel like after working with a couple of businesses I'll have a better grasp of the concepts and the skills required to do my own thing, that being freelancing.

Photography

The last thing that I want to do is to start photography. The main reason why this is later is because I want to get plenty of experience in the world of business, growing my credentials and allowing myself the time to adapt to all that I will need to know before learning a new kind of art. I also hope to have done plenty of photoshop before this time so that I can tailor what I've done to something new. The last thing is that by the time that I want to start photography I'll both have the money and time to do so.

Other Ideas

I wish that I could say that I've thought of many other plans, but I will be coming up with more plans in the near future. The one thing that comes to mind is doing more things tailored to Web Design, the only thing is I don't have a good idea of what I would be doing.
